> I had the opportunity to be a mentor at the NepTech Docker Workshop. It was a good 2 day session where I got to share my knowledge about Docker to the community members and help them get started with containerization.

<!--more-->

### Overview of the Workshop
The workshop was designed to provide a hands-on experience with Docker, covering the fundamental concepts. It was structured as an interactive session with as much practical demonstrations as possible. The key topics covered in the workshop included:
- Introduction to Docker and Containerization
- Setting up Docker Environment
- Docker Images and Containers
- Dockerfile Basics
- Managing Containers
