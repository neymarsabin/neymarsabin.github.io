The talk was structured to provide a comprehensive understanding of Blockchain Technology, starting from the basics to more advanced concepts. The key topics covered in the talk included:
- Introduction to Blockchain Technology
- How Blockchain Works
- Types of Blockchains
- Smart Contracts - EVM, Opcodes, Gas Fees
- Decentralized Applications (DApps)
- ERC20 Token Demo
